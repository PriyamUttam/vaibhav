    Neha Malhotra

    Founder and Managing Partner


    Ms. Neha Malhotra is Managing Partner at MeritX Ventures®. She is an internationally recognised startup mentor and investor readiness expert, angel investor and on the advisory boards of international startups.

    She has rich experience supporting startups across India, UAE and Canada on their 'zero to one' journey and preparing them holistically for pitching to investors and accelerators. Her previous experience includes being an investment banker and strategy consultant with KPMG, Deloitte and Axis Bank. Ms. Neha is a gold medalist with a Master's degree in Economics & Management from the London School of Economics. She is a public speaker and visiting professor at various business schools, including her alma mater, the LSE's India Centre.

    She is a passionate learner, bibliophile and a mixed media artist in her spare time.
